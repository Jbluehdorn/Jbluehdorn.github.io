# Best of the Worst Wheel

Build me a wheel that works like the wheel found in ../kahaiz_wheel

This wheel needs to have every episode of Best of the Worst from RedLetterMedia on youtube on it

It will cycle through all the episodes and randomly pick one episode to watch

It will have distinct differences due to its size

Design wise, I was to only show 5 or so episodes on a half arc to simulate a much larger wheel but it will be random episodes

We will need to scrape with a node script which runs on build to get any new episodes that we don't have loaded (could be multiple and will be many for the first time we run it)

The wheel will just spin with a button and can slow at an exponential rate
