Welcome to the Rock of Bral

You are an adventurer living on the Rock of Bral — a mile-long asteroid city drifting through Wildspace, the vast emptiness between worlds. Think of it as a frontier port town, except the frontier is outer space, the port accepts ships that sail between stars, and the town is built on a flying rock.

The Rock is home to roughly 25,000 souls: merchants, sailors, pirates, nobles, refugees, thieves, priests, and everything in between. People come here from dozens of different worlds, and almost everyone is here for one of three reasons: to trade, to hide, or to find opportunity.

You are in the third category.

What You Know

The Rock is divided into three districts — see Maps & Visuals for full maps.

Low City — the docks, the warehouses, the rough taverns. This is where ships arrive, cargo moves, and deals get made away from official eyes. The Rat's Maze is here, and you've been warned to stay out of it unless you have business.

Middle City — the commercial heart. The Grand Bazaar will sell you nearly anything. The Temple Row will offer blessings, answers, or guilt in equal measure. The Gambler's Den will take your coin either way.

High City — where the powerful live. The Palace sits at the top, home to Prince Andru, the ruler of the Rock. The Noble Quarter surrounds it. You probably don't have business up there yet.

The Laughing Beholder

Your home base is The Laughing Beholder — the most famous tavern on the Rock, run by a beholder named Large Luigi. It's neutral ground, it's loud, and its Adventurers' Board is where work finds people like you.

Wildspace

Beyond the Rock is Wildspace — airless, gravity-less, and full of things that want to kill you or rob you (sometimes both). Ships sail it using magical spelljamming helms. If you ever get access to a ship, there's a whole solar system out there to explore: other asteroids, planets, ancient ruins, and stranger things.

Further still, past the edge of the solar system, is the Phlogiston — a turbulent, multicolored medium between crystal spheres. It's highly flammable. Don't bring an open flame.

Who's In Charge

Prince Andru rules the Rock. He's pragmatic and not unkind, but he is absolutely self-interested. The constabulary enforces the law in Middle and High City. In Low City, law is more of a suggestion.

The major power players you've heard of:

  • House Traken — richest merchant house on the Rock
  • The Juggler's Guild — the thieves' guild; they run the Low City underworld
  • The Arcane — alien traders who sell spelljamming helms; strictly neutral in all things
  • The Seekers — the adventurers' guild; friendly to people like you
