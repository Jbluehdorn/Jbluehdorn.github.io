West Marches Player Guide

Bral Stars runs West Marches style. Here's what that means for you.

Schedule

Event When Duration
Main Session 3rd Tuesday of each month ~3 hours, 5:30 PM PST
Workshop (optional) Thursday following the main session ~1 hour, 5:30 PM PST

Players drive scheduling. Post in Discord when you want to play. No fixed party — any combination of PCs can go on any adventure.

Monthly Hook Voting

At the start of each month, the DM posts available plot hooks in Discord. Players vote on which one to pursue at the next main session.

  • Hooks are posted and voted on in Discord (check pinned messages)
  • The winning hook is the focus of the scheduled session
  • Unvoted hooks remain available for future months
  • Parties can still pursue off-board leads, but the voted hook takes priority for the main session

Workshop Thursdays (optional)

Between main sessions. Use them for:

  • Downtime activities (crafting, research, training, shopping)
  • NPC conversations that don't need the full table
  • Loose ends from the previous session
  • Character or party admin

No obligation to attend. It's an outlet for smaller-scope play.

Recaps Required

Whoever wants XP and loot logged posts a short recap to Discord after the session. Doesn't have to be long — a paragraph and a highlight reel is fine.

The World Is Persistent

  • NPCs remember what the party did.
  • Locations change after interactions.
  • Factions track reputation over time.
  • Each session's outcome shapes future sessions. If you ignore a thread, it keeps moving without you. If you push on one, it reacts.

Emergent Play

The DM isn't writing a plot. The DM is running situations — factions, NPCs, and locations that all want things. The story comes from what you do, not from a script.

  • No railroad. No "right answer." Consequences are real.
  • Have goals. Take action. Follow curiosity. Passive PCs get passive stories.
  • The DM plays the world honestly and lets the pieces collide.

Downtime

Between sessions, each character may pursue one downtime activity per week of in-game time. Message the DM with what your character is doing. Common options: crafting, research, working a job, training, shopping, socializing, laying low.

Communication

  • Discord is the source of truth — pinned hook messages, party formation, recaps, downtime.
  • This site is the reference — factions, NPCs, locations, character creation.
  • The DM is available for one-on-ones if you want to develop backstory, negotiate a downtime outcome, or plan a session prep.
