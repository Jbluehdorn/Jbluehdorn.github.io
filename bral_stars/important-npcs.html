Important NPCs

Important NPCs

The people on the Rock you're most likely to run into, get in trouble with, or need to talk to. Not exhaustive — new faces will surface every session.

Large Luigi

Race: Beholder · Where: The Laughing Beholder · Alignment: Chaotic Neutral

A beholder who decided running a tavern was more interesting than world domination. Gregarious, dramatic, and genuinely enjoys good company. Absolutely no patience for troublemakers, whom he disintegrates without ceremony. Knows every face, name, and debt on the Rock. See Important Locations for the tavern.

Prince Andru

Race: Human · Title: Prince of Bral · Residence: Starhaven

Ruler of the Rock. Shrewd, pragmatic, and very self-interested. Inherited young, outmaneuvered his rivals early. Presents as genially accessible. Persistent rumor says he commands a secret police of clowns — officially deniable, universally suspected.

Ronald McDonald

Where: Around town — everywhere and nowhere

The Rock's most recognizable clown. Pure-white face. Deeply unsettling energy under the friendly veneer. If the clown rumors about the Prince are true, this is who leads them. Beloved by children. Distrusted by adults who've been paying attention.

Ringmaster Blorg

Where: The Arena · Faction: The Cirque

A crocodile. In a ringmaster's outfit. Runs the Cirque. Doesn't speak (he's a crocodile). Nobody can explain why they respect him. Everyone does.

Captain Barnacle

Where: Low City bars · Reputation: Beloved former pirate

Spins tall tales of piracy at every Low City bar that'll have him. Absolutely capable of violence if pushed. Tips extravagantly. Never drinks the drinks people buy him.

Jawless Jerry

Where: Low City, after dark · Status: Local cryptid

A coyote with no jaw. Old dockworker superstition: if you see her, the next day brings either great fortune or great disaster. Never a middle outcome. Nobody knows what she actually is.

Professor DC

Where: Mages' Hall · Role: Wizard

One of the many wizards at the Mages' Hall. Or is he? People who've dealt with the Hall long enough swear they can't quite tell the faculty apart anymore.

Steve

Where: Starhaven, at his post

A palace naval guard currently having a very obvious existential crisis. Won't say why. Won't stop staring at the middle distance. Big Wendy's fan.

Warden Bigboots

Where: Starhaven barracks

Warden of the palace barracks. Got the job because of his family. Has size-2 shoes and a voice that squeaks when he's excited. Unhinged in ways that will not be limited to the barracks.

Jennifer Coolidge

Where: The Boroughs · Role: HOA President for the Hammers

The HOA president of the Hammers. Wants everything to look real nice. Hosts long, long meetings.

Kay Aren

Where: The Boroughs · Role: Assistant to Jennifer Coolidge

Jennifer's assistant. Actually runs the operational side of the HOA. Universally trusted. Universally underestimated.

Mafia Don Jon

Where: The Donjon estate, High City · Faction: The Donjon Crime Family

Rumored head of an old-money crime family. Rumored to have his hands in half the schemes on the Rock. Rumored, rumored, rumored. Nobody has proved anything.
